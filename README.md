This repository contains a portfolio of data analytics projects covering data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical hypothesis testing, A/B testing, business metrics analysis, and dashboards. Projects span e-commerce, healthcare, real estate, food delivery, entertainment, and nonprofit sectors.

Completed as part of the Yandex Practicum Data Analytics program and the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate (Coursera).

## Projects

| Project | Industry | Description | Stack |
|---------|----------|-------------|-------|
| [**A/B Test: Payment Funnel**](./ab_test_payment_funnel) | E-commerce | **Goal:** Evaluate impact of redesigned payment funnel on purchase conversion<br>**Key findings:** Statistically significant 37% drop in conversion rate in test group<br>**Recommendations:** Do not implement; diagnose checkout flow drop-off points | Python (pandas, numpy, scipy, statsmodels, matplotlib, seaborn) |
| [**A/B Test: Recommendation Algorithm**](./ab_test_recommendation_algorithm) | Entertainment / Media | **Goal:** Assess impact of new recommendation algorithm on user engagement<br>**Key findings:** 3.4% statistically significant increase in successful sessions in test group<br>**Recommendations:** Roll out new algorithm to production | Python (pandas, scipy, statsmodels, matplotlib, seaborn) |
| [**Hospital Length-of-Stay Analysis**](./hypothesis_testing_medical_center) | Healthcare | **Goal:** Assess hospitalization duration patterns and deviations from medical benchmarks<br>**Key findings:** Statistically significant deviation from the 7-day benchmark (mean ≈ 8.7 days); ICU/neonatal departments average ~45 days<br>**Recommendations:** Expand bed capacity; differentiate by admission type and department | Python (pandas, numpy, scipy, matplotlib, seaborn) |
| [**Donor Behavior Analysis**](./donation_activity_analysis) | Nonprofit | **Goal:** Identify behavioral patterns to improve donor motivation and retention<br>**Key findings:** 14% of donors generate 68% of donations; bonus users show ~18x higher average donation activity<br>**Recommendations:** Expand bonus programs; prioritize repeat donor retention | SQL (PostgreSQL) |
| [**Bike-Share Usage Patterns**](./eda_bike_share_usage_patterns) | Transportation / Mobility | **Goal:** Identify behavioral differences between members and casual riders to inform marketing strategy<br>**Key findings:** Members show commuter patterns; casual riders show leisure patterns<br>**Recommendations:** Offer flexible/seasonal memberships; promote commuting benefits | R (tidyverse, ggplot2, lubridate) |
| [**Seasonal Demand & User Behavior Analysis**](./eda_hypothesis_testing_event_ticketing) | Entertainment / Ticketing | **Goal:** Identify seasonal demand patterns to support promotional campaign planning<br>**Key findings:** Autumn seasonality drives growth; ~80% mobile orders; concerts and theater dominate<br>**Recommendations:** Prioritize mobile optimization; focus autumn campaigns on concerts and theater | SQL (PostgreSQL), Python (pandas, numpy, scipy, statsmodels, matplotlib, seaborn), Yandex DataLens |
| [**Food Delivery Dashboard**](./business_metrics_food_delivery_service) | Food Delivery | **Goal:** Calculate and visualize key business metrics for food delivery service<br>**Key findings:** Low retention (~4-5% Day 7); two restaurants drive LTV; 8.6% AOV increase May-June<br>**Recommendations:** Improve retention via notifications and loyalty programs; promote high-LTV items | SQL (PostgreSQL), Yandex DataLens |
| [**Moscow Food Service Market**](./eda_food_service_market) | Food & Hospitality | **Goal:** Support market entry decisions for Moscow food service sector<br>**Key findings:** Cafés, restaurants, coffee shops show strongest demand; Central District has highest density; ratings show a positive association with price category<br>**Recommendations:** Launch café/coffee shop/restaurant in Central or Western Districts; target mid-range pricing | Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn, phik) |
| [**Real Estate Market Analysis**](./real_estate_market_analysis) | Real Estate | **Goal:** Identify high-demand segments and seasonal patterns in Saint Petersburg real estate<br>**Key findings:** Autumn peak activity; SPb has 1.5-2x higher price per m²; smaller units sell faster<br>**Recommendations:** Plan market entry for autumn; prioritize smaller, affordable inventory in SPb | SQL (PostgreSQL), Yandex DataLens |
| [**Startup Funding Analysis**](./eda_startup_funding) | Venture Capital / Startups | **Goal:** Inform market entry and acquisition strategy based on funding dynamics (1999-2014)<br>**Key findings:** 88.8% operate in mass markets; technology, medical, internet, apps fastest-growing; returns exceeded invested capital for debt_financing, private_equity<br>**Recommendations:** Focus on fast-growing markets; prioritize debt financing and private equity for ROI | Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn) |
